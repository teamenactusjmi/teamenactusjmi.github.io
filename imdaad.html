Urdu, Help
One of the most important things you can do on this earth is to let people know they are not alone.

Women of Jasola community, hailing from socio-economically deprived part of the society are targeted through the Project ‚ÄòImdaad‚Äô which aims to support them by generating revenue and essential livelihood standards. This project is a sustainable business model simulated by a naturally crafted healthy and refreshing drink. The model is based upon a supply-chain system which primarily functions from Kerala to Delhi.

MISSION

To provide a healthy alternative to carbonated drinks and to give employment to underprivileged women of the Jasola community through the manufacture of the drink.

OVERVIEW

The Indian Sarsaparilla roots, a medicinal herb with effective cooling and body purifying properties, are used to prepare the drink. The syrup is extracted from the roots and produced by the Jasola community. It has an improved shelf life because the drink is now being tetra packed. The process of manufacturing the syrup commences with the plucking of roots followed by washing, soaking, boiling and finally extracting the syrup from the roots while being mindful of the hygiene. It is then synthesised by the Jasola community using either milk or water.The community also plays an integral role in selling of the drink through the stalls which are setup in college festivals or corporate events.
